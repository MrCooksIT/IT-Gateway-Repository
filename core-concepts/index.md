This section covers the theoretical foundations of Information Technology as outlined in the CAPS curriculum for grades 10-12.

## What You'll Learn

Core concepts form the theoretical backbone of IT knowledge. These concepts appear primarily in Paper 2 (Theory) examinations but understanding them is essential for practical work as well.
